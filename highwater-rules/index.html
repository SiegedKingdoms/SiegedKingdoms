Introduction

Highwaters is a temporary server between iterations. The world is mostly ocean sparsly populated with island, oil rigs and other structures.

It is a near lawless land, so keep your wits up and keep your location only known to your closest allies.

Read everything carefully, certain items and blocks are dissalowed.

WARNING: THIS SERVER CONTAINS STRONG DRUG AND ALCOHOL MISUSE, GAMBLING, ACTS OF TERROR, DISCUSSIONS, AND ACTS OF WAR, AS WELL AS SUICIDE

Highwaters Rules

  1. Stealing/Griefing is prohibited if the team/person is offline. The only exception to this rule is of corpses that can be looted at any time if found.
  2. No Eureka Balloons or Ship engines are to be used. Ship engines can be crafted but not used as they are used to make the Rotational Engine.
  3. Abusing any duping bugs or anything of VS/Eureka.

General Rules

  1. Spamming / Mass Pinging any chat or forum space within the Sieged Kingdoms community, including personal DMs with other players, is prohibited.
  2. For moderation purposes, please keep all public chats in English. Our other supported languages are Spanish, Portuguese, and Japanese; however, speak English where possible.
  3. Attempts to make the server unplayable with a structure or toxic behavior is disallowed.
  4. NSFW Roleplay, images, or displays on the server or public chat spaces will not be tolerated. Lewd State and crew names are allowed to an extent, such as “The Cum Zone,” but nothing overly grotesque and containing profanities.
  5. Prolonged toxicity or bullying is punishable, up to moderators discretion
  6. Discrimination against server members based on race, gender identity, sexual orientation, or other comparable characteristics is strictly prohibited.
  7. Exploiting bugs and using hacked clients is not permitted, for example, negating fall damage via a bug, duplication, flying, speed, pie-ray, x-ray, or other forms of cheating.
  8. Advertising of other Minecraft or Discord servers anywhere in the server or private DMs to other members is prohibited.
  9. Doxxing, Pedophilia/lolicon, and other seriously degenerate behavior will result in an immediate permanent ban and a report to Discord and respective authorities.
  10. No builds, symbols, or other displays with unfriendly intent.
  11. Using a 3rd party mod or other client modification to provide yourself with an advantage over other players is prohibited.
  12. Using an external program to provide yourself with an advantage over other players is prohibited.

Follow The Discord terms of service at all times when in the Discord server space. Sieged Kingdoms’ moderation team has the final say regarding handing out punishments.

If you see or hear a player breaking any of the regulations and do not report it, you will be banned for 25% of the offender's time. If you suspect cheating, hacking, or admin abuse, please DM @Izunatta on Discord.
